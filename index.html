About Me

Hey there! My name is Alanis and this is my portfolio website.

I am currently a student at Cab Calloway School of the Arts. As a major in digital media, I am expeienced in photography, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, HTML 5, and CSS 3. I also take classes for visual art and I am trained in the use of graphite pencils, watercolor paint, and acrylic paint. I have been taught about the elements of art and principles of design to create beautiful compositions.

My goal for the future is to have an artistic career in which I can create beautiful artwork for everyone to enjoy.
